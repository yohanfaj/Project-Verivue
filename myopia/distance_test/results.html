Disntace Test - Results

The results of the Distance Test are calculated as the following:
  • Lines 1 to 4: Heavy Myopia is diagnosed (visual acuity below 50%)
  • Lines 5 or 6: Slight Myopia is diagnosed (visual acuity between 50% and 80%)
  • Lines 7 or 8: Myopia is not diagnosed for the tested eye (visual acuity above 80%)
