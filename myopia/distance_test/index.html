Specifications before starting

The distance at which to perform the test depends on the size and resolution of your screen. You must therefore calibrate the test layout.

To do this, first measure the length of the red figure below on your screen:

Multiply the result by 100 and position yourself at this distance from your screen.

Example: measured length = 42 mm, test distance = 42mm x 100 = 4200mm = 4.2 meters.

Please cover your RIGHT eye with your palm to continue. You must keep this position during all of the upcoming tests.
